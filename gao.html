Hung Yen's Rice Production Potential

Hung Yen cultivates approximately 145,000-155,000 hectares of rice annually with a yield of 130-132 quintals/ha, producing a stable output of nearly 1 million tons to meet domestic and export demands.

The province applies advanced techniques like alternate wetting and drying irrigation, UAV rice care, VietGAP and Organic standards, with over 200 processing facilities capable of handling 200,000 tons/year.

Key Rice Production Zones

Hung Yen develops high-quality rice areas with main varieties like TBR39 and Bac Thom 7 in Tien Hai district, and organic rice-rươi ecological models in Dong Hung.

Districts like Quynh Phu, Vu Thu, and Kien Xuong specialize in premium rice varieties such as Tam Xuan, Hom Rau, and Nep Keo, with many products achieving OCOP certification.

Modern Technology in Rice Production

Hung Yen implements alternate wetting and drying techniques, uses UAVs for precise spraying and fertilizing, saving water and reducing pesticide use by 30% while improving production efficiency.

The province deploys VietGAP and Organic models with water-nutrient sensor systems and over 200 processing facilities with 200,000 tons/year capacity to ensure rice quality.

BRAND DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY

1. Multimedia Communication Campaign

Organize the "Hung Yen Rice Journey" video contest (3-5 minutes with English subtitles) showcasing modern production processes and farmers' stories.

Develop brand slogans like "Hung Yen Rice - Purely Vietnamese. Globally Loved." for consistent use across media channels and product packaging.

2. Professional Brand Identity System

Design a unified identity with the logo "Hung Yen Rice - Green Gold of Vietnam" combining rice stalks and Red River imagery, using green and gold colors symbolizing sustainable development.

Develop premium packaging with VietGAP/Organic certifications and QR traceability codes, helping consumers easily recognize and trust the products.

3. Digital Platform for Export Support

Build a multilingual e-commerce website introducing Hung Yen rice brand, integrating online ordering and 24/7 customer support, optimized for mobile devices.

List products on international e-commerce platforms like Alibaba and Amazon to reach global importers and consumers.

4. "Faces of Hung Yen Rice" Imagery Campaign

Produce artistic photo series in rice fields, processing plants, and family kitchens to depict the journey from grain to delicious rice bowl.

Select diverse brand ambassadors including farmers, chefs, students to spread Hung Yen rice's image to various target groups.
