USEFUL INFORMATION

Tips to help you have a complete spiritual pilgrimage

SPIRITUAL TOURISM EXPERIENCE

A spiritual trip is not just a journey of discovery but also an opportunity to purify the soul. For a meaningful and smooth trip, visitors should note the following important points.

Clothing when visiting temples should be discreet and polite, preferring elegant colors. Bring small change for offerings, but avoid overuse of votive papers. When entering temple areas, keep quiet, don't speak loudly, don't point at statues. Learn about local customs and rituals in advance to avoid violating taboos.

Ideal Time

Spring (Jan-Mar)

Season of big festivals, cool weather, many unique cultural activities

Summer (Apr-Jun)

See golden rice fields, warm weather, convenient for travel

Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Cool climate, beautiful natural scenery, less rain

Preparation

  • Discreet, polite clothing (prefer dark colors)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Small change for offerings (exchange in advance)
  • Camera or phone (silent mode in temples)
  • Umbrella/raincoat for rain or sun
  • Personal medicine (if needed)

Local Specialties

  • Lang Kenh rolled rice pancakes (North Hung Yen)
  • Quynh Coi fish soup (South Hung Yen)
  • Ninh Binh crispy rice
  • Ne Chau sticky rice cake
  • Me So sweetened sticky rice
